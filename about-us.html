About Sahayata

Wish to spend money on charity, but unsure about whom to donate?

With us, help a child get his/her desired education; give your unused clothes, shoes, books, or toys to the ones in need; help us drive, give quality health treatment to the underprivileged; or just help us raise funds that positively impact vulnerable and low-income people of the society.

Our volunteers provide an impressive array of services that span the spectrum from every age, race or community. Our mission is to strengthen individuals, and empower families by providing a path to a brighter future.

For nearly 5 years, we have responded rapidly and effectively to the needs of our local partners and our communities. We utilize our national reach to help families in India accelerate the health and financial equity they deserve.
